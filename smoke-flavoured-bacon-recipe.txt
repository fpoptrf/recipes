Ingredients:
    - 200g block of bacon
    - 10g cherry wood chip

Directions:
    1. Put wood chip on aluminum foil sheet, then Place them on chinese pot.
       Place rosting net to the pot, lay block of bacon on it. Cover the pot with lid.
    2. Low heat for 30 minutes until the bacon is colored light brown.
    3. Remove the bacon from the pot and place it cool and wind place for 30 minutes to
       reduce excesive smoke flavour.
